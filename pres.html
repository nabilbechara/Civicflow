Improving communication between citizens and municipalities
01 / 12
The Problem

Municipal services remain
fragmented and opaque

  • Services handled through physical visits, phone calls, and paperwork
  • Citizens don't know where to submit, what's required, or how long it takes
  • Employees struggle to organize, prioritize, and follow up on requests
  • Citizens lack visibility after submitting a request
  • Limited transparency, tracking, and accountability
02 / 12
Current Gaps

What existing workflows
are missing

  • No centralized platform for citizens, employees, and admins
  • Weak request tracking and limited status updates
  • No clear role-based access control
  • Documents handled manually — risk of loss or misplacement
  • Escalated or high-priority requests are hard to identify
  • Citizens repeatedly contact municipality for updates
03 / 12
The Solution

What CivicFlow
improves

  • Centralizes services, news, staff info, and request tracking
  • Online request submission with document uploads
  • Status tracking and full request history for citizens
  • Staff dashboards for reviewing, updating, and assigning requests
  • Priority levels and escalation workflows
  • Role-based access for transparency and accountability
04 / 12
User Roles

Four roles, clear permissions

Citizen

  • Register, log in, view services
  • Submit requests with file uploads
  • Track own requests and activity
  • View notifications and news
  • No admin access, no view of other citizens' data

Municipal Employee

  • Access employee dashboard
  • View and review assigned requests
  • Update status, add activity messages
  • Process and move requests forward
  • Cannot manage platform-level users

Municipality Admin

  • Admin dashboard for their municipality
  • Manage requests and assignments
  • View user management features
  • Manage news and announcements
  • Oversee workflow and progress

Super Admin

  • Platform-wide administrative controls
  • Oversee multiple municipalities
  • Manage platform settings and users
  • Highest access level and responsibility
